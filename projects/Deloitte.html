I joined the innovative team at Deloitte Design Studio within Deloitte India, Bangalore, in 2018. Throughout my tenure, I had the privilege of collaborating closely with esteemed clients such as Unilever, FAB LC, Vodafone MVB, CEAT, and Deloitte Digital itself. As an Assistant Manager, I contributed to various projects, leveraging my expertise as a UX Consultant in Technology Consulting. From June 2019 to February 2020, I served in this capacity, and prior to that, from March 2018 to May 2019, I held the role of User Experience Consultant, focusing on product strategy and design in the Bengaluru area.

Working closely

For a deeper insight into my work, feel free to reach out for detailed samples.

Role

  • Working closely with clients & Stakeholders
  • Facilitating client requirement workshops with stakeholders of varying levels of client organizations
  • Contributing to and helping evolve internal design pattern library for each product with Visual designer
  • Documenting client requirements as user requirements, and interaction design annotations on wireframes, prototypes, and other low-fidelity representations of the user experience
  • Creation of behavioral personas
  • Development of user scenarios, mental models and user journey maps
  • Extrapolating product vision as a synthesis of primary research and client vision
  • High-level information architecture of a single, or multichannel, digital ecosystem, represented in a site map or application map
  • Qualitative, contextual inquiry including designing a study, recruiting and facilitating research interviews
  • Hiring for the growth of the team also known as blitz

Designing for e-commerce across omnichannel customer journeys has taught me to solve for complex systems outside just the digital space.

Takeaways from this role

  • Defining the problem and the scope early on is key
  • Going broad with ideas is helpful but tracking progress is necessary
  • Get in front of users to validate ideas as soon as you can
  • Never stop trying new things and strategies
